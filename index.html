1   Introduction

I am an experienced tax consultant in various branches of tax law and jurisdictions. Throughout my professional career I have assisted oilfield service companies in matters of international tax planning, individuals in assessing the best juridictions for their needs, business and individuals in avoiding typical hurdles due to red taping, etc.

My main areas of expertise are International Tax Law, VAT, and Portuguese Personal and Corporate Income Tax.

Additionally, I program in JavaScript and Solidity as a hobby, and I am a crypto enthusiast.
