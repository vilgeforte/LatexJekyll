1   Introduction

I am an experienced tax consultant in various branches of tax law and in various jurisdictions. However, my main areas of expertise are International Tax Law, VAT, and Portuguese Personal and Corporate Income Tax. Notwithstanding the last paragraph, if I had to choose a single branch of tax law to serve as the basis of my professional activity, I would choose International Tax Law. International Tax Law is undoubtedly the most exciting and the most complicated branch of tax law, since one needs to know several languages and jurisdictions in order to be able to advise the client in the best way.
